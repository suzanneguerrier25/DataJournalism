Bird Calls: A Project

Designed to bring awareness to the injured birds in NYC, and a helpful service that isn't necessarily common knowledge by NYC residents. Go to the about page for more information, or look below for a breakdown by borough.

{% include 'NYC_boroughs.svg' %}

KEY: the higher the opacity, the more bird calls in the area.

In NYC boroughs, the amount of bird calls varied, with the most in Manhattan and Brooklyn, specifically Manhattan had {{manbirdnumtempl}} bird calls, and Brooklyn had {{brkbirdnumtempl}} bird calls. Conversely, the other boroughs had much less, as Queens, Staten Island, and the Bronx had {{qubirdnumtempl}}, {{sibirdnumtempl}}, and {{brnxbirdnumtempl}} calls respectively.


My takeaways from this have been:

  1. There are a lot of injured birds in NYC. I initially thought there'd be around 20 per borough per year, but instead, it's a lot more!
  2. A lot of birds are being found injured in Manhattan and Brooklyn. I thought that it would be more even around boroughs.
  3. There are quite a few calls being made already- but more can always be made, and the number of actual birds injured most likely isn't reflected in these numbers
